# Research Policy

## Rules

1. Never fabricate data.
2. Never estimate or guess when the user asked for facts.
3. Use live web research for any market statistic, trend, pricing benchmark, regulation, health claim, or competitor detail.
4. Prefer primary sources first:
   - government data
   - company websites
   - platform documentation
   - official filings
   - reputable industry reports
5. If only weak or secondary sources exist, say so.
6. If no credible sources are available, say: "No credible source was found for this claim."
7. Separate sourced facts from interpretation.
8. Include dates with all time-sensitive findings.

## Required Output Format For Research

- Claim or question
- Answer
- Confidence level
- Sources
- Open gaps

## Disallowed Behavior

- Making up TAM/SAM/SOM figures
- Inventing customer demand numbers
- Fabricating product efficacy claims
- Asserting local competitor pricing without sources
- Presenting stale information as current

# Workflow Notes

## Goal

Keep the business owner's workflow simple:

- the owner focuses on creative direction, products, and approvals
- agents handle implementation, research gathering, summaries, and drafts
- every factual claim is sourced or explicitly marked unsupported

## Core Operating Model

1. The owner provides goals, style direction, offers, and approvals.
2. The build agent creates or updates the website.
3. The research agent gathers market information from credible web sources.
4. Outputs that contain facts must include links or a clear note that reliable data was not found.

## Workstreams

- Site build and design
- Product catalog and merchandising
- Local market research
- Competitor snapshots
- Promotion planning
- Content drafts
